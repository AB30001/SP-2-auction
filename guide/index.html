Guide to Using Bidora

Creating an Auction Listing

To create an auction, you need to be a registered user. Not registered yet? Register here.

After logging in, go to the Profile tab and open the My Auctions section.

Click the Create New Auction + button, which will take you to the listing creation page. Fill in all the necessary details, then click Create.

You can monitor the progress of your listings and view bids by accessing your My Auctions section in the Profile tab over time.

Bidding on Auctions

To participate in bidding, you must be a registered user. Not registered yet? Sign up here.

Once logged in, navigate to the Auctions tab and search for the listing that interests you.

Click the View button to see all details about the listing, including the option to place a bid. You'll also find the bidding history and auction expiration date here.

Place your bid, and then track your activity from your Profile page, where you can view your entire bidding history.
